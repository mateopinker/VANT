# Safety Notes

Use this page for stable safety boundaries and checklist summaries. Keep detailed records in `docs/safety/`.

## Standing Rules

- Define test objectives before energizing propulsion or applying loads.
- Keep bystanders clear during structural, propulsion, and flight tests.
- Record test setup and acceptance criteria before running a test.
- Stop and document unexpected damage, vibration, heat, or loosened hardware.

## Pre-Test Checklist

- Test objective written
- Area clear
- Emergency stop or safe shutdown method identified
- Fasteners and joints inspected
- Battery or energy source checked
- Camera or notes ready for evidence
