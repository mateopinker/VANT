## All the basic information about the current state of the aircraft

- Aircraft type: fixed-wing UAV / VANT
- Takeoff: probably hand launch
- Landing: belly landing using small skids
- Payload: avionics only for now
- Wingspan target: around 1.5 m, adjustable depending on lift
- Wing: fiberglass laminated with PLA printed core/structure
- Fuselage: lightweight 3D printed body
- Estimated mass target: 3.2–4.0 kg provisional
- Main risk: excessive mass from 3D printed parts
